Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Cognisance National Esports Championship?

The Cognisance National Esports Championship is a competitive gaming event hosted by MIT-ADT University, bringing together gamers from across the nation to compete in various popular esports titles.

Who can participate in the championship?

The championship is open to all students and esports enthusiasts above age 18, regardless of their skill level. Participants must register in advance to secure their spot in the competition.

How do I register for the event?

Registration can be completed through our official website. Participants must fill out the " Player "registration form and there is no entry fee. Make sure to check the registration deadline to ensure your participation.

What games will be featured in the championship?

Battle Ground Mobile India (BGMI)

Will there be prizes for the winners?

Yes, winners will receive exciting prizes, including cash rewards, gaming gear, and certificates. Details regarding the prize distribution will be announced during the event.
